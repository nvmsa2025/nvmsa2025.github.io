Call for Posters and Demos

The NVMSA 2025 Posters and Demos session provides an opportunity to present early-stage work and receive feedback from both the non-volatile memory systems community and the real-time/embedded systems community from RTCSA 2025, which is co-located with the conference. We invite both research and industry presentations in all areas listed in the call for papers. This year, for the first time, a Best Poster and a Best Demo will be awarded!

 

Abstract Submission Guidelines

  • Poster/demo abstracts have at most 2 pages of technical content, including all text, figures, tables, appendices, references, etc.
  • The submitted extended abstracts are subject to single-blind reviews.
  • Authors should use an IEEE two-column conference style with 10pt font size, on US Letter paper size according to the IEEE conference paper proceedings format.
  • Please add a "Poster:" or "Demo:" prefix to your title.
  • The accepted abstracts will be published in the proceedings of NVMSA 2025.
  • Submission web page at:
    • Submission Portal

Conference Registration and Presentation Requirements

  • A poster/demo extended abstract requires a student registration or a full registration. A full registration can cover a conference paper (either full or short) and/or a poster/demo extended abstract.
  • NVMSA 2025 only accepts in-person presentations. Exceptions require the General Co-Chairs' approval based on documentary proof.

Important Dates:

  • Submission deadline: June 21, 2025 AoE
  • Acceptance notification: July 4, 2025
  • Camera-ready deadline: July 11, 2025
