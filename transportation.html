Transportation

✅ Changi Airport → Park Avenue Rochester

  • MRT: Changi Airport → Tanah Merah → Buona Vista

    • Take the train from Changi Airport MRT Station (CG2) to Tanah Merah MRT Station (EW4), then transfer to the East West Line towards Tuas Link MRT Station (EW33). Get off at Buona Vista MRT station and exit via Exit C

    .
  • Taxi: Changi Airport → Park Avenue Rochester (138637)

    • Taxis are available for hire at the taxi stands in the Arrival areas of Terminals 1, 2, 3 and 4. The taxi fare from Changi Airport to Park Avenue Rochester is approximately SGD 30-40. The journey takes about 25-35 minutes

    .
  • Private hire car: Changi Airport → Park Avenue Rochester (138637)

    • Passengers can also book private hire cars from Grab (Recommended), Gojek (Recommended), Zig, Tada Mobility or Ryde for a ride out of the airport. Uber is not available in Singapore. The fare is similar to that of a taxi.
